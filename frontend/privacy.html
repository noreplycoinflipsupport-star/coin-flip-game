Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide during registration: name, email address, phone number, and account preferences. We also collect game history, transaction records, and usage data to improve the Platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain your account.
  • To process deposits, withdrawals, and game transactions.
  • To communicate with you about your account (OTP, reset emails, etc.).
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse.
  • To improve the Platform and user experience.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely. Passwords are hashed and not stored in plain text. We implement reasonable security measures but cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with law enforcement if required by law. We may use third-party services (email, hosting) that process data on our behalf.

5. Cookies

We use local storage for authentication tokens and theme preferences. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

6. Your Rights

  • You can request a copy of your data.
  • You can request deletion of your account and data.
  • You can update your profile information.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. After account deletion, data may be retained for legal or audit purposes for a reasonable period.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified via the Platform or email.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us through the Platform's support channels.
